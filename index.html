All about horses!

Basic info

Horses are gentle giants, they love to jump, gallop, and many other fun things. They are strong creatures, and be careful around them, their kicks could nearly kill you if they are strong enough. .

Why horse riding is a great sport!

  • its fun!!
  • helps with balance!
  • helps bond with your horse!

Why horse riding is a qualified sport

Horse riding is a qualified sport for many reasons, horse riding is in the olymics, and has been an olymic programme for over 100 years. this equestrian sport is one of the hardest, most dangerous, and most expensive sport. Most riders have trained for years to gain such skill, and it is never easy. alot of people think that its easy because you just sit there, its not that simple. You have to keep your balance, and if you dont, you are at risk of falling off. You have to gain strength to control your horse because a horse is much bigger and stronger.
